Take a look at what makes us unique...

A maximum 3 mile radius

A smaller radius allows you to meet people that are within walking distance. This makes it possible to meet someone in the same public area such as an event, downtown or at college.

Immediate notification when someone likes you

Meet people in the present moment. Immediacy puts the power in your hands rather than swiping continuously searching for a match. We want you to know when someone is interested in you.

Matches & likes expire in 24 hours

Customizable Radius

You can adjust your radius to the size of your concert, venue, park, downtown area, game, event and more. This allows you to meet people with similar interests that are nearby.

On/Off Mode

Turn Proxii on when you want to be seen and match with others. Turn Proxii off when you don't. This feature is made to support your lifestyle.
