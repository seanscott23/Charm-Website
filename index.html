Take a look at what makes us unique...

A maximum 1 mile radius

A smaller radius allows you to meet people that are within walking distance. Making it possible to meet someone in the same downtown area, festival, college or any place you are.

Immediate notification when someone likes you

Meet people in the present moment. Immediacy puts the power in your hands rather than swiping continuously searching for a match.

Limit of 10 messages each

We added this to help everyone avoid the 'texting trap'. Too often people end up getting to know someone top to bottom all virtually. This forces the hand to make sure a meetup happens if both parties desire.

Drop a location pin

Easily let your match know where you with a quick pin drop of your location. At a certain bar? Grabbing pizza at your favorite spot? Just walking in downtown? Send a pin.
